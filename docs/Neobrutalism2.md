# Neobrutalism 2

**Theme ID:** `neo2`

A second take on neo-brutalism with a cooler beige palette and a more typographic, editorial tone. Where Neobrutalism 1 uses warm cream and yellow, this variant goes cooler and adds **Bebas Neue** for display headlines alongside Space Grotesk for body copy.

## Visual Character

Cooler beige background (`#F2EDE4`) vs. the warmer cream of v1. Three accent colors instead of two: electric blue (`#0000DF`), red (`#FF0000`), and yellow (`#FFFF00`). Hard shadows are retained but organized into a token system — `elev-1` through `elev-4` with 2/4/6/8px offsets respectively.

## Typography

- Display: Bebas Neue (ultra-condensed, all-caps)
- Body: Space Grotesk
- Mono: JetBrains Mono

## Key Design Traits

- Elevation token system (`elev-1` to `elev-4`) for consistent hard shadow sizing
- Three accent colors vs. Neobrutalism 1's two
- Bebas Neue display font adds more editorial/poster character
- Same zero-radius, black-border foundation as v1
- Beige base instead of cream — slightly cooler, more "print" feel

## Difference from Neobrutalism 1

Both share the hard-shadow, black-border brutalist foundation. v2 is more structured (elevation tokens), typographically richer (Bebas Neue), and has a cooler color temperature.

## Use When

You want neo-brutalism with a more editorial, magazine-layout feel — or when the warm cream of v1 feels too cozy and you want something sharper.
