# Neobrutalism

**Theme ID:** `neo`

A neo-brutalist design system with warm cream backgrounds, ultra-heavy typography, and hard black offset shadows. Inspired by the brutalist web revival — raw, confrontational, and deliberately "undesigned."

## Visual Character

Warm off-white background (`#fffbf0`) with pure black borders (`#000000`) on every element. The signature visual is the **hard shadow**: cards and buttons are offset 4–8px down-right with a solid black shadow and no blur — creating a stacked paper effect. Yellow (`#ffdd00`) and red (`#ff4747`) are the accent colors.

## Typography

- Body/Display: Space Grotesk (weight 800 for headings — aggressively heavy)
- Mono: Courier New

## Key Design Traits

- Zero border-radius — every corner is a right angle
- Hard offset shadow (`box-shadow: Xpx Xpx 0 #000`) instead of soft drop shadows
- Bold black borders on all containers and inputs
- Uppercase labels and heavy tracking on headings
- Interactive: buttons visually "press" by reducing the shadow offset on `:active`

## Use When

You want a bold, memorable design that breaks from the standard SaaS aesthetic. Especially effective for portfolios, landing pages, and design system showcases.
